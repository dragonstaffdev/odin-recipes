Shrimp Scampi Pasta

Ingredients

Simple Recipe Ingredients

  1. 2 tsp kosher salt
  2. 1/2 lb linguine pasta
  3. 3 Tbsp unsalted butter
  4. 2 Tbsp olive oil
  5. 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  6. 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  7. 1/2 cup dry white whine
  8. 1 lb frozen shrimp, defrosted and peeled
  9. 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  10. Juice of 1 large lemon
  11. 2 Tbsp parsley, chopped

Complex Recipe Ingredients

  1. 1 tsp + 1 Tbsp kosher salt
  2. 1 tsp baking soda
  3. 1 lb fresh shrimp, peeled (shells reserved) and deveined
  4. 1 cup dry white wine
  5. 1 bay leaf
  6. 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  7. 1/2 panko bread crumbs
  8. 8 Tbsp butter, divided
  9. 1/2 lb fresh fettuccine pasta
  10. 3 Tbsp olive oil
  11. 6-8 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  12. 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  13. 1 large shallot, minced
  14. To taste freshly ground black pepper
  15. Juice of 2 lemons
  16. 2 tsp fresh tarragon, chopped
  17. 1 Tbsp fresh marjoram, chopped
  18. 3 Tbsp parsley, chopped
  19. Zest of 1 lemon
